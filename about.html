Bridging Heritage & Modernity

Art Taara is more than a marketplace; it is a dialogue between the rich legacy of Indian art and the contemporary collector.

Our Story

Founded in Delhi NCR and Chandigarh, Art Taara was born from a simple observation: Indian art is vast, beautiful, and often inaccessible to the modern homeowner.

We work directly with emerging and established artists to curate collections that are not just decorative, but soulful. Whether it is a vibrant Gouache on paper or a serene oil canvas, every piece tells a story of culture, craftsmanship, and quiet beauty.
