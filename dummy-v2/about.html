About Art Taara

Art Taara is an artist-led curatorial platform focused on Indian art practices — both traditional and contemporary.

We work closely with artists to present their work in ways that are respectful, contextual, and sustainable. Each project begins with conversation: about the artist’s practice, the client’s space, and the role art is meant to play.

Rather than operating at scale, we choose to work slowly and deliberately, building long-term relationships with artists, collectors, designers, and institutions.
