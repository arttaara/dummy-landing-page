About Art Taara

Art Taara is an artist-led marketplace dedicated to curating exceptional Indian artworks, both traditional and contemporary. We bridge the gap between talented artists and discerning collectors, designers, and institutions.

Our platform celebrates India's rich artistic heritage while embracing modern expressions. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected for its authenticity, craftsmanship, and the unique story it tells.

We work closely with artists to ensure they receive fair recognition and compensation for their work, while providing our clients with access to exclusive, high-quality art that transforms spaces and enriches lives.
